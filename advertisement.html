About Us 

                  
      
     Lakshmi Krishna Naturals was founded by P. Mohan. It was launched on March 5, 2018, as a natural homemade cosmetic product. It was first started for their family purpose due to skin and hair issues, 
     
     but ultimately ended up as a startup business in his home itself. They shared the natural homemade products with the neighbors around their house. As it shows great results for them, it was then started 
     
     a product business. Till now Lakshmi Krishna Naturals has achieved 511K followers on Instagram and 1.81 L subscribers in youtube. It has 9 shops in and around Tamil Nadu and it is also available in all 
     
     leading online shopping stores for natural skincare and hair care products on Indian websites such as Flipkart, Amazon, Meesho, and Jio Mart and you can also purchase it from our official website too. 

     Around 4 lakh happy customers are available both online and offline. As a result, Lakshmi Krishna Naturals offers high-quality products to the people, and it also gives positive results without any side
     
     effects. The five major categories for which Lakshmi Krishna Naturals products are manufactured and made available for purchase are skin care, hair care, health care, body care, and baby care.
